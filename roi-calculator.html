How we calculate

Every number updates live as you change inputs. The math is straightforward and matches standard B2B demand-gen planning:

  • Closed deals needed = Revenue goal ÷ Average contract value
  • Leads needed = Closed deals ÷ (MQL-to-close rate ÷ 100)
  • Total investment = Leads needed × $25 CPL
  • CAC = Total investment ÷ Closed deals
  • ROI = (Revenue goal − Investment) ÷ Investment × 100
  • Monthly lead volume = Leads needed ÷ Timeline in months

Key assumption: SignalARC's base rate of $25/lead for the 25-lead minimum tier. Larger prepay packs and subscriptions reduce effective CPL by up to 10%. Conversion rates reflect 2024–2025 benchmarks for content-sourced MQLs with a proper email nurture program in place.

What this calculator doesn't account for: Ad spend, sales team cost, CRM/marketing automation licenses, or content creation costs. It's a pipeline-level view of lead economics only.
