## The 6 personas

### P1 — Financial Services & Insurance
**Persona:** The Compliance-Minded CMO

**Primary titles:** VP Marketing, Director of Demand Generation, Director of Marketing, Head of Growth
**Secondary titles:** CMO, VP of Digital Marketing, Director of Product Marketing, Field Marketing Director

**Apollo filters:**
- **Industries:** Banking, Financial Services, Insurance, Investment Management, Venture Capital & Private Equity, Capital Markets, Accounting
- **Keywords:** fintech, credit union, regional bank, wealth management, commercial insurance
- **Employee range:** 200 – 5,000
- **Revenue:** $50M – $5B
- **Location:** United States, Canada
- **Seniority:** VP, Head, Director
- **Department:** Marketing

**Pain points:**
- Compliance-heavy content approval slows go-to-market
- Crowded LinkedIn advertising
- Small target universe of decision-makers
- Long regulatory sales cycles

**Hook:** *"Compliance-friendly B2B lead gen that works in regulated finance and insurance."*

**Best content angle:** Benchmark reports, compliance playbooks, peer case studies with concrete ROI numbers.

**Channel:** LinkedIn + Email

- **Revenue:** $25M – $2B
- **Location:** United States, Canada
- **Seniority:** VP, Head, Director

## Execution priority

**Month 1–2:** Start with **P1 personas** (Financial Services, Healthcare, HR). These have the highest alignment between buyer sophistication, content receptivity, and your existing assets.

**Month 3–4:** Validate P1 performance (15%+ reply rate, 2%+ meeting booked rate). Add **P2 personas** (Manufacturing, Logistics) as performance allows.

**Month 5+:** Layer in **P3** (Commercial Real Estate) once you have a peer case study from one of the other industries. CRE buyers want social proof from adjacent industries.

## Outreach sequence (swipe from the Playbook)

All 6 personas can be emailed with the same 5-email nurture sequence from our public [B2B Lead Gen Playbook](/b2b-lead-gen-playbook.html). For SignalARC's own outbound:

- **Email 1:** Value drop (send them the relevant industry section of the Benchmark Report)
- **Email 2:** One specific question about their biggest demand gen pain
- **Email 3:** Case study from their industry (even if hypothetical/aggregate)
- **Email 4:** Teaching email on what most [their title]s get wrong
- **Email 5:** Soft 15-min conversation ask

Customize the `[brackets]` per persona using the pain points and hook from this doc.

Re-review this doc quarterly. Specifically refresh:

- **Company size ranges** — as SignalARC's ACV/CPL evolves, upper/lower bounds shift
