Why We Give Every New Client 25 Free B2B Leads.

SignalArc — April 2026 — 5 min read

When you start a new content syndication program with SignalArc, you receive 25 free verified B2B leads delivered to your inbox after kickoff. No strings attached, no catch, no minimum commitment required to claim them.

We get asked about this a lot. Why would we give away leads for free? The answer is simple — and it says something important about how we think about client relationships.

We Built This System for Ourselves First

The content syndication process we run for clients is the exact same process we used to build SignalArc's own pipeline. We didn't acquire it from a third party or license a platform — we built it from scratch to solve our own lead generation problem.

That means we know exactly what it feels like to receive your first batch of leads, review the contact data, and start thinking about how to follow up. We know the uncertainty of a new program. And we know that the fastest way to eliminate that uncertainty is to show you what the leads actually look like before you've committed to anything significant.

We eat our own cooking. The system we're selling you is the same system we use. The 25 free leads are a demonstration of exactly what you're buying, not a marketing gimmick.

What the 25 Free Leads Include

Your free leads are delivered with the same verified contact data as every lead in our paid programs:

  • Full name
  • Verified work email address
  • Job title
  • Company name and size
  • Industry
  • Geography

These are real people who have engaged with B2B content relevant to your solution area. They are not purchased list contacts, they are not scraped data, and they are not unverified form fills. They are the same caliber of lead you'll receive every week if you continue as a client.

How to Make the Most of Your First 25 Leads

When your leads arrive, treat them like the intent signals they are:

  1. Review the titles and companies first — assess fit against your ICP before doing anything else
  2. Follow up within 48 hours — the follow-up window is real; don't let good leads go cold
  3. Reference the content in your outreach — personalized follow-up that acknowledges what they downloaded converts significantly better than generic openers
  4. Track the responses — even from a small sample of 25, you'll get a clear read on lead quality and conversion potential

What Happens After the 25 Leads

If the leads are good — and we're confident they will be — the conversation about a full program becomes very easy. You've seen the product. You know the data quality. You know what your sales team will be working with.

If for any reason the leads don't meet your expectations, we want to know. Our targeting is only as good as the feedback we get from clients, and we take quality seriously enough to iterate on targeting before you commit to a paid program.

The 25 free leads aren't a hook. They're the beginning of a business relationship we want to earn. If what you see in those first leads reflects the pipeline quality your business needs, we'd love to keep building it with you.
